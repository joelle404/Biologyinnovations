Welcome to the Biology Student Creations Website

We are 11th-grade students from TSG St. Josef School for Girls in Bethlehem, working together in our biology class under the guidance of our teacher, Miss Angelique Rock. This project was inspired by our passion for promoting natural beauty and health remedies. Our goal is to collect and share simple, effective recipes made from natural ingredients that are safe and easy to use.

We worked on this project during class, combining creativity and teamwork to make these recipes that benefit anyone looking for sustainable and natural solutions. Through this initiative, we aim to encourage others to embrace nature's benefits and make informed choices about their personal care.

this website developed by the students Christine Matar and Maria Rishmawi.
